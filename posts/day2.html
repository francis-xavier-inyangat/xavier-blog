Week 2 HTML & CSS:The hill climbing is simple

Not difficult at all. All you need to know is that there are tags <\tag> and
each tag has properties and seen part/content. Much like
different people. People are all different, each has
its own properties, and each has its own seen part. From here you just need to "meet" the
different people and get to know them.

In my opinion, you could learn HTML in a day or two(at least the basics), after that, start learning CSS and learn it while improving HTML. When you get some understanding of responsive design and how things work, you can start learning JS. Of course, it's up to you, but in my opinion you should start and focus on one thing at a time instead of learning everything at the same time, and dedicate at least an hour a day for learning. You could check out #100DaysOfCode movement, it is pretty cool and can motivate you!
